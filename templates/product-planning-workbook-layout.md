# Product Planning Workbook Layout

Use this layout for product initiation planning workbooks.

## Sheets

1. `意向产品`
   - category and product idea
   - reference image placeholder
   - price positioning
   - customer group
   - functions and unmet needs
   - material, packaging, color/style
   - target purchase cost, selling price, margin
   - launch month and first batch

2. `市场分析`
   - sales trend
   - seasonality
   - listing age
   - rating and review barriers
   - price band
   - brand concentration
   - keyword trend
   - market conclusion

3. `竞品分析与优化策略`
   - representative ASIN
   - brand, price, monthly sales
   - listing selling points
   - positive review keywords
   - negative review pain points
   - benchmark and optimization direction

4. `SWOT分析`
   - S: internal strengths and reusable capability
   - W: missing capability and cold-start barriers
   - O: market gaps and trend windows
   - T: failure modes and external threats

5. `ABA排名【季度】`
   - keyword
   - monthly search volume
   - monthly purchases
   - product count
   - average price
   - PPC
   - supply-demand ratio
   - click concentration
   - SellerSprite rank
   - strategic note

6. `成本试算`
   - exchange rate
   - purchase cost
   - product size and weight
   - site price
   - platform commission
   - fulfillment/FBA fee
   - first-leg freight
   - refund/defect rate
   - ad ratio
   - gross margin and gross profit

7. `销售计划`
   - logistics method
   - lifecycle stage
   - price stage
   - first three month units
   - revenue
   - gross profit
   - gross margin

8. `数据来源`
   - MCP files/tools
   - source reports
   - manual assumptions
   - calculation notes
   - data gaps

## Rule

Do not use the workbook to invent evidence. Every planning conclusion should point back to MCP data, a source report, a cost assumption, or a clearly marked manual assumption.
